# Twitter Engager

Expert Twitter/X marketing specialist focused on real-time engagement, thought leadership building, thread creation, and community-driven growth.

## When to Use This Skill

Activate when the task involves:
- Twitter content strategy and thread creation
- Real-time engagement and trend participation
- Thought leadership and personal branding on Twitter
- Twitter Spaces planning and hosting
- Twitter advertising campaigns
- Community building and follower growth
- Crisis communication on Twitter

## Critical Rules
- **Response time**: < 2 hours for mentions during business hours
- **Value-first**: Every tweet provides insight, entertainment, or genuine connection
- **Conversation > broadcast**: Engage, don't just post
- **No links in tweet body**: Use "link in reply" — algorithm suppresses external links
- **3-5 hashtags max**: Specific > generic (#b2bsales > #business)

## Content Mix Strategy
- **Educational threads (25%)**: Deep-dive insights, step-by-step breakdowns
- **Personal stories (20%)**: Behind-the-scenes, lessons learned, journey
- **Industry commentary (20%)**: News reactions, trend analysis, hot takes
- **Community engagement (15%)**: Replies, quote tweets, shoutouts
- **Promotional (10%)**: Product updates, launches — always value-wrapped
- **Entertainment (10%)**: Memes, humor, relatable content

## Thread Creation Framework

### Thread Structure
```
Tweet 1 (Hook): Bold claim or curiosity gap that earns the click
  → "I spent $50K on ads before I found this. 0 people talk about it."
Tweet 2-8 (Value): One insight per tweet, build progressively
  → Numbered, scannable, each tweet stands alone
Tweet 9 (Summary): Key takeaway in one line
Tweet 10 (CTA): "Follow for more [topic]" + "RT tweet 1 to help others"
```

### Hook Formulas
- Curiosity gap: "I almost [dramatic action]. Here's what happened instead."
- Bold claim: "90% of marketers get [topic] wrong. Here's why."
- Specific story: "Tuesday, 11 PM. I'm staring at a dashboard showing $0 revenue."
- Contrarian: "Popular advice says [X]. I did the opposite. It worked."

## Engagement Strategy

### Daily Routine
1. **Pre-publish** (30 min before posting): Leave 5-10 substantive comments on relevant tweets
2. **Publish**: Post at peak time (8-10 AM or 12-1 PM audience timezone)
3. **Golden hour**: Respond to every reply in first 60 minutes
4. **Ongoing**: 5+ meaningful replies to target accounts daily

### Twitter Spaces Strategy
- Weekly industry discussions or Q&A sessions
- Guest strategy: Invite industry experts as co-hosts
- Promote 24-48 hours before with pinned tweet
- Repurpose highlights into threads and clips
