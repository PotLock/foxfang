# LinkedIn Content Creator

Expert LinkedIn marketing specialist focused on thought leadership, professional networking, B2B lead generation, and authority building.

## When to Use This Skill

Activate when the task involves:
- LinkedIn content strategy and thought leadership positioning
- B2B lead generation and social selling
- LinkedIn company page management
- Employee advocacy and personal branding programs
- LinkedIn advertising (Sponsored Content, InMail, Lead Gen Forms)
- Professional networking and relationship building

## Critical Rules
- **No external links in posts**: LinkedIn suppresses link posts — use "link in comments"
- **Professional tone**: Authentic but polished — no clickbait or gimmicks
- **Document posts dominate**: PDF carousels get 3x more reach than text-only
- **Engagement reciprocity**: Comment on others' posts before/after publishing
- **Consistency**: Post 3-5x per week minimum for algorithm momentum

## Content Strategy Framework

### Content Pillars
- **Thought leadership (30%)**: Industry insights, contrarian takes, predictions
- **Personal stories (25%)**: Career lessons, failures, behind-the-scenes decisions
- **Educational (25%)**: How-to guides, frameworks, step-by-step breakdowns
- **Social proof (10%)**: Case studies, client wins, testimonials (humble-brag format)
- **Promotional (10%)**: Product/service announcements — always value-wrapped

### Post Formats (by reach potential)
1. **Document/Carousel posts**: 3-5x more reach — use for guides, tips, data
2. **Text + image**: Strong engagement with personal photos or data visuals
3. **Text-only**: Works for personal stories and hot takes (use line breaks!)
4. **Video**: Native video preferred, keep under 3 minutes
5. **Polls**: High engagement but low quality — use sparingly for research

### Writing Framework
```
Line 1: Hook (curiosity gap or bold statement)
Line 2: [blank line — forces "see more" click]
Line 3-10: Value delivery (short paragraphs, line breaks)
Line 11: Key takeaway or lesson
Line 12: CTA (question to drive comments)
Line 13: 3-5 relevant hashtags
```

### Hook Formulas for LinkedIn
- "I [did/quit/started X]. Here's what happened."
- "Unpopular opinion: [contrarian take about industry]"
- "3 things I wish I knew about [topic] 5 years ago:"
- "Everyone talks about [common thing]. Nobody talks about [real thing]."
- "[Big number] [result] in [timeframe]. Here's exactly how:"

### LinkedIn Algorithm Signals
- **Dwell time**: Long-form content that keeps users reading
- **Early engagement**: Comments in first 60 minutes are critical
- **Conversation quality**: Replies with 15+ words count more
- **Profile authority**: SSI score, connection quality, posting consistency

## B2B Social Selling Framework

### The LEADS Method
1. **Listen**: Monitor target accounts' activity and interests
2. **Engage**: Comment meaningfully on prospects' posts (3-5 per day)
3. **Attract**: Publish content addressing their pain points
4. **Direct**: Send personalized connection requests (after engagement)
5. **Serve**: Provide value via DM before any pitch

### LinkedIn Sales Navigator Integration
- Build saved searches for ideal customer profiles
- Track lead engagement with your content
- Use InMail templates based on engagement history
- TeamLink for warm introductions
