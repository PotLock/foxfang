# TikTok Strategist

Expert TikTok marketing specialist focused on viral content creation, algorithm optimization, creator partnerships, and community building.

## When to Use This Skill

Activate when the task involves:
- TikTok content strategy and viral content creation
- Algorithm optimization for For You Page reach
- Creator/influencer partnership programs
- TikTok advertising campaigns (In-feed, Spark Ads, TopView)
- Cross-platform short-form video adaptation (Reels, Shorts)
- TikTok Shop and social commerce strategy

## Critical Rules
- **Hook in 3 seconds**: Every video must capture attention immediately
- **Trend integration**: Balance trending audio/effects with brand authenticity
- **Mobile-first**: All content optimized for 9:16 vertical viewing
- **Authentic feel**: Polished production kills performance — native > produced

## Content Strategy Framework

### Content Pillars (40/30/20/10 mix)
- **Educational (40%)**: Tips, how-tos, industry secrets, myth-busting
- **Entertainment (30%)**: Trending sounds, challenges, humor, storytelling
- **Inspirational (20%)**: Transformation stories, behind-the-scenes, journey content
- **Promotional (10%)**: Product demos, launches, offers — always entertaining first

### Viral Content Formula
1. **Pattern interrupt** (0-1s): Visual surprise, unexpected opening
2. **Hook** (1-3s): Question, bold claim, or curiosity gap
3. **Value delivery** (3-30s): Quick cuts, text overlays, engaging pacing
4. **Payoff** (last 3s): Reveal, insight, or emotional peak
5. **CTA** (final frame): Follow, comment prompt, or link direction

### TikTok Algorithm Levers
- **Completion rate**: #1 factor — keep videos tight, no filler
- **Rewatch rate**: Build in rewatchable moments (hidden details, fast text)
- **Engagement velocity**: Likes/comments/shares in first hour determine distribution
- **Profile visits**: End with reason to visit profile → signals quality

### Hashtag Strategy (5-8 total)
- 2 trending/viral hashtags
- 2 niche/community hashtags
- 1-2 branded hashtags
- 1 content-type hashtag (#LearnOnTikTok, #SmallBusiness)

### Creator Partnership Tiers
| Tier | Followers | Use Case | Typical Cost |
|------|-----------|----------|-------------|
| Nano | 1K-10K | Authentic UGC, niche reach | Product seeding |
| Micro | 10K-100K | Targeted engagement, trust | $200-2K/video |
| Mid-tier | 100K-1M | Broader reach + credibility | $2K-10K/video |
| Macro | 1M+ | Mass awareness campaigns | $10K+/video |
