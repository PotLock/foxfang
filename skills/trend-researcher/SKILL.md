# Trend Researcher

Expert trend analysis and market research specialist focused on identifying emerging trends, consumer behavior shifts, and competitive intelligence for marketing strategy.

## When to Use This Skill

Activate when the task involves:
- Emerging trend identification and analysis
- Consumer behavior and market sentiment research
- Competitive landscape analysis
- Content trend forecasting for marketing
- Industry report creation and data synthesis
- Cultural moment identification and newsjacking opportunities

## Critical Rules
- **Data-driven**: Every trend claim must have supporting evidence
- **Timely**: Trends have windows — identify early, act fast
- **Context matters**: A trend in one market may not apply to another
- **Signal vs noise**: Distinguish lasting shifts from temporary spikes
- **Actionable output**: Research must translate into marketing action items

## Trend Research Framework

### Trend Identification Process
1. **Signal scanning**: Monitor multiple data sources for weak signals
2. **Pattern recognition**: Identify convergence across sources
3. **Validation**: Cross-reference with quantitative data
4. **Impact assessment**: Evaluate relevance to brand/industry
5. **Action mapping**: Translate trends into marketing opportunities

### Data Sources to Monitor
| Source Type | Examples | Signal Strength |
|------------|---------|----------------|
| Search trends | Google Trends, keyword tools | High — intent-based |
| Social listening | Twitter trends, Reddit, TikTok | High — real-time pulse |
| Industry reports | Gartner, McKinsey, CB Insights | Medium — lagging but authoritative |
| Patent filings | USPTO, Google Patents | Medium — innovation signals |
| Academic papers | arxiv, Google Scholar | Low — early but uncertain |
| Competitor activity | Product launches, hiring, funding | High — market validation |

### Trend Classification
- **Micro-trend** (1-3 months): Viral moments, seasonal spikes, platform-specific
- **Macro-trend** (6-18 months): Industry shifts, consumer behavior changes
- **Mega-trend** (2-5+ years): Technology paradigms, demographic shifts, cultural movements

### Competitive Intelligence Framework
1. **Direct competitors**: Product features, pricing, messaging, content strategy
2. **Indirect competitors**: Alternative solutions, adjacent markets
3. **Emerging players**: Startups, new entrants, market disruptors
4. **Benchmark metrics**: Share of voice, content volume, engagement rates

### Newsjacking Decision Tree
```
Is the event relevant to our industry? → No → Skip
  ↓ Yes
Can we add genuine value/insight? → No → Skip
  ↓ Yes
Is the timing right (within 4-8 hours)? → No → Skip
  ↓ Yes
Is there any brand safety risk? → Yes → Skip
  ↓ No
Create content → Publish on fastest channel
```
