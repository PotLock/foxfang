# Social Media Strategist

Expert cross-platform social media strategist for building brand authority, managing campaigns, and growing engaged communities across LinkedIn, Twitter, Instagram, and professional networks.

## When to Use This Skill

Activate when the task involves:
- Cross-platform social media strategy and campaign coordination
- B2B social selling and professional audience development
- Multi-platform content calendar and editorial planning
- Employee advocacy and brand ambassador programs
- Thought leadership positioning across channels
- Social media performance analysis and optimization

## Cross-Platform Strategy Framework

### Platform Strengths
| Platform | Best For | Content Style | Posting Frequency |
|----------|----------|---------------|-------------------|
| LinkedIn | B2B, thought leadership, recruiting | Professional, insightful | 3-5x/week |
| Twitter/X | Real-time news, engagement, community | Conversational, witty | 3-7x/day |
| Instagram | Visual brand, lifestyle, commerce | Aesthetic, curated | 4-7x/week |
| TikTok | Viral reach, Gen Z, entertainment | Authentic, trend-driven | 3-5x/week |
| Reddit | Community trust, authority, feedback | Value-first, authentic | 2-3x/week |

### Unified Messaging Framework
1. **Core message**: One brand story adapted to each platform's culture
2. **Content cascade**: Primary content on strongest platform → adapted versions elsewhere
3. **Engagement loops**: Drive cross-platform following and community overlap
4. **Consistent hashtags**: Branded + industry hashtags across all channels

### Campaign Planning Template
```
Campaign: [Name]
Objective: [Awareness / Engagement / Traffic / Leads / Sales]
Platforms: [List with format per platform]
Duration: [Start - End]
Audience: [Segment per platform]
Content: [# posts per platform, formats]
Budget: [Organic vs paid split]
KPIs: [Primary + secondary metrics]
```

### Content Mix by Platform
- **LinkedIn**: Company updates (20%), thought leadership (30%), employee spotlights (15%), industry insights (25%), promotional (10%)
- **Twitter**: News commentary (25%), engagement/replies (25%), threads (20%), personal stories (15%), promotional (15%)
- **Instagram**: Brand aesthetics (30%), behind-the-scenes (20%), UGC (20%), educational (15%), promotional (15%)
