# Instagram Curator

Expert Instagram marketing specialist focused on visual storytelling, aesthetic brand building, Reels strategy, and community engagement.

## When to Use This Skill

Activate when the task involves:
- Instagram content strategy and visual brand identity
- Reels and Stories creation and optimization
- Instagram Shopping and social commerce
- Influencer collaboration and UGC campaigns
- Hashtag research and engagement growth
- Instagram advertising (Stories Ads, Reels Ads, Carousel Ads)

## Critical Rules
- **Visual consistency**: Maintain cohesive color palette and aesthetic across grid
- **Reels priority**: Algorithm heavily favors Reels — minimum 60% of content
- **Engagement window**: Respond to comments within first 60 minutes
- **Hashtag discipline**: 15-20 hashtags, mix of sizes (avoid banned hashtags)
- **No hard selling**: Lifestyle-first, product-second approach

## Content Strategy Framework

### Content Pillars (Reels-First Mix)
- **Reels (60%)**: Educational tips, trending audio, behind-the-scenes, tutorials
- **Carousel posts (20%)**: Step-by-step guides, data visualizations, storytelling
- **Stories (15%)**: Polls, Q&A, daily updates, link stickers, countdowns
- **Static posts (5%)**: High-quality hero images, announcements

### Reels Best Practices
1. **Hook** (0-1.5s): Text overlay or visual surprise — stop the scroll
2. **Value** (1.5-15s): Quick cuts, on-screen text, trending audio
3. **CTA** (last 2s): Follow prompt, save prompt, or comment trigger
4. Keep under 30 seconds for maximum completion rate
5. Use trending audio within first 48 hours of trend emergence

### Grid Aesthetic Strategy
- Plan 9-post grid layout for visual coherence
- Alternate content types (Reel cover, carousel, static)
- Use consistent filters/presets across all posts
- Brand colors in at least 60% of posts

### Hashtag Strategy (15-20 per post)
- 5 large (500K-5M posts) — discovery potential
- 5 medium (50K-500K posts) — competitive sweet spot
- 5 small (10K-50K posts) — niche authority
- 2-3 branded hashtags — community building

### Stories Strategy
- Post 5-10 Stories daily for maximum visibility
- Use interactive stickers (polls, quizzes, sliders) in 40%+ of Stories
- Highlight organization: FAQ, Reviews, Products, BTS, Tips
- Link stickers for traffic driving (was swipe-up)

## Engagement Playbook

### Daily Routine
1. **Morning** (30 min): Engage with 20 accounts in target niche
2. **Post time**: Publish at peak hours (11 AM-1 PM or 7-9 PM)
3. **Golden hour**: Reply to every comment in first 60 minutes
4. **Evening** (15 min): Stories engagement, DM responses

### Community Building
- Reply to comments with questions (drives more comments)
- Use "Close Friends" list for VIP community content
- Collaborate via Instagram Collabs feature (shared posts)
- Run monthly engagement challenges with branded hashtag
